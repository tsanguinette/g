Our Menu

We freshly prepare Cod, Haddock, Plaice, and Mackerel with a crisp coating of batter. Add mushy peas or a buttered roll for a small fare. Among fish and chips we also serve Sausage, burgers, squid, shark, chicken, and pies. If you fancy something more classic, try our scrumptious Cod Goujon. As always, just order food with us and we'll deliver it to you as you as enjoy your drink at The Birdcage Pub across the road.
