About Us

The Grosvenor Fish Bar is long established, 90 years, as a traditional fish and chip shop. We have refurbished and now serve the usual chip shop fare, plus an interesting selection of our own creations. Our seating downstairs is for up to 50 people. You can eat in or take away for the same price. If you want to have a beer or a glass of fizz with your meal, just order food with us and we'll deliver it to you as you as enjoy your drink at The Birdcage Pub across the road.
